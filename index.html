Welcome!

Advanced Computer Science is taken at Morgantown High School and after completing AP Computer Science A. During the first semester you will work with different data structures and practice writing your own classes. In the second semester, you will move on to some more difficult projects such as game development, machine learning, and SQL. There will be a final project at the end of each semester and they will challenge you into using the skills you've acquired while taking this course. The syllabus for the class is found below and other resources such as textbooks and solutions can be found on the site as well. Good luck!
